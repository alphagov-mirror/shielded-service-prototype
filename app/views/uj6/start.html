Get support if you’re ‘shielding’ because of coronavirus

Use this service if you have a medical condition which means you’re classed as being clinically extremely vulnerable to coronavirus.

You can use this service to:

  • get access to priority supermarket deliveries if you live in an area that’s at Local COVID Alert Level: very high
  • confirm whether you’d need additional support if you were told to ‘shield’s
  • update your details - for example, your address

‘Shielding’ means staying at home and minimising contact with people outside your household.

You can register yourself, or on behalf of someone else.

This is a new service. You will need to register again, even if you got support while shielding between March and August 2020.

If you’ve already got priority supermarket deliveries, you’ll keep them.

Register or update your details

Before you start

You’ll also be asked for your NHS number. You can find it on any letter the NHS has sent you or on a prescription.

Register or update your details by phone

Contact your local authority to register or update your details by phone.

If you don’t live in England

This service is available in England. There's separate guidance for:

  • Northern Ireland
  • Scotland
  • Wales
