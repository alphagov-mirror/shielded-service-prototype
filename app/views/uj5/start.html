Get support if you’re ‘shielding’ because of coronavirus

You can use this service to:

  • ask for help with getting priority supermarket deliveries or meeting your basic care needs
  • update your details, for example your address

’Shielding’ means staying at home as much as possible and keeping visits outside to a minimum.

Who should use this service

Use with this service if:

  • you have a medical condition which means you’re classed as being clinically extremely vulnerable to coronavirus
  • you’ve been told by your GP or hospital clinician that you’re clinically extremely vulnerable and need to ‘shield’

This is a new service. You’ll need to register again, even if you got support while you were shielding during the period from March to August 2020.

Register or update your details

Before you start

You’ll also be asked for your NHS number. You can find it on any letter the NHS has sent you or on a prescription.


There’s guidance from:

  • Northern Ireland
  • Scotland
  • Wales
