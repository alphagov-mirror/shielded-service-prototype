What happens next

We’ll check your details to make sure you’re eligible for support. You’ll only get support if you asked for it.

If we need to confirm any details, you’ll get a call from the National Shielding Service. The number that will show on your phone is 0333 3050466.

Getting support

If you're eligible and you said that you do not have a way of getting basic supplies at the moment we will start sending you a a weekly box of basic supplies.

We'll also share your details with participating supermarkets to help them prioritise you for theiry delivery services.

If you do not already have an account with a supermarket delivery service, set one up now. If you're concerned about not being able to get a delivery slot, you can set up accounts with more than one supermarket.

It may take some time before you start getting support. Contact your local authority if you need supplies urgently and cannot rely on family, friends or neighbours.

If you support needs change

You can tell the delivery driver if you want to stop getting the weekly box of supplies.

If your support needs change, go through the questions in this service again.

There's guidance on what you should do as someone who's especially vulnerable to coronavirus.
