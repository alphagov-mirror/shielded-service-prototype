What information about you is shared with supermarkets

We share your name, address and contact details with supermarkets so they can find you in their system and offer you priority deliveries.

Supermarkets will keep this information until it’s clear that clinically extremely vulnerable people will not need this kind of support in future.

  • Morrisons
