Get help with your heating bill

You can use this service to:

  • Request a Winter Heating Voucher if you are clinically extremely vulnerable to coronavirus and claiming working age benefits.

You can register yourself, or on behalf of someone else.

Start now

Before you start

You’ll be asked for your NHS number. You can find it on any letter the NHS has sent you or on a prescription.

You’ll also be asked for your National Insurance Number. You can find your National Insurance number on your payslip, P60, or letters about tax, pensions and benefits.


If you need help using this service

Contact your local authority if you need help registering or updating your details

If you do not live in England

This service is available in England. There's separate guidance for:

  • Scotland
  • Wales
