What is your National Insurance Number?

We ask for your National Insurance Number to check you're claiming working age benefits.

If you’re registering for someone else, enter the National Insurance Number for the person who is clinically extremely vulnerable.


It’s on your National Insurance card, benefit letter, payslip or P60. For example, ‘QQ 12 34 56 C’.
