Get coronavirus support as a clinically extremely vulnerable person

Register with this service if either:

  • you live in England, and have a medical condition which means you’re classed as being clinically extremely vulnerable to coronavirus
  • you live in England, and you’ve been told by your GP or hospital clinician that you’re extremely vulnerable and need to ‘shield’

If you need it, you’ll be able to:

  • get a weekly box of basic supplies
  • get priority for supermarket deliveries
  • ask for help meeting your basic care needs

Register whether you need help at the moment or not.
